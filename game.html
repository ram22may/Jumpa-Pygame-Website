JUMPA

Game Mechanics:

Fire Projectile:
In Jumpa, enemies can launch a fire projectile toward the player. This projectile travels in a straight path and damages anything it hits. If the player is struck by the fire projectile, their health points (HP) are reduced by 25. Players must avoid or dodge these attacks to survive longer and prevent their HP from reaching zero.

Gold Mine:
In Jumpa, players can explore a gold mine where they gather valuable gold resources. By mining deposits scattered throughout the area, players earn gold that can be used as in-game currency. This gold is essential for survival, as it allows players to purchase potions that restore health or provide helpful effects. The more gold a player collects, the better equipped they become to face tougher challenges and enemies.

Potion:
The potion is an essential healing item in the game. When used, it restores 25 HP to the player. Potions are very important during combat, especially when fighting strong enemies or the boss. Players must manage their potions wisely because they are limited.

Pickaxe:
The pickaxe is the required tool for mining gold in the gold mine. Without the pickaxe, the player cannot collect resources. It allows the player to break rocks and gather gold efficiently, which is used for survival upgrades like potions.

Sword:
The sword is the main weapon used by the player. It is the only equipment capable of damaging enemies and the boss. Every attack with the sword is important because it is the key to defeating strong enemies and completing the game.

Ogre:
The ogre is the main boss that appears when the player reaches a score of 25. It is a powerful enemy that attacks by launching three projectiles downward at the same time. If the player is hit, they lose 25 HP. To defeat the ogre, the player must dodge attacks, use the sword effectively, and rely on potions for healing.
